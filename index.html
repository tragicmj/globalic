About Us

Global IC is one of the most successful International Education Agencies in Eastern India. Our mission is the career Success of our Students and we pride ourselves on achieving this, with a success rate of 100% over our 14 years in business.

Our highly tested method of working with students to create a career plan, and build a foundation that is best suited to their abilities is our key to turning students into professionals of tomorrow capable of facing challenges overseas.
