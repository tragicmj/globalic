MBBS CHECKLIST :

  • Payment of Global IC Service Charge.
  • A Bank Draft for Rs.2,000/- +18% GST in favour of “The Secretary, Medical Council of India, New Delhi"
  • Whether candidate’s name, Father’s name, phone no. & purpose of application has been written on the reverse side of DD/Pay order.
  • MCI Application form in original.
  • Proof of Nationality or Overseas Citizen of India.
  • Three self attested copies of Pass Certificate as well as Mark sheet of 10th Class or equivalent Board examination along with copy of Transfer Certificate/Migration Certificate in case change of School.
  • Three self attested copies of Pass Certificate as well as Marksheet of 11th Class or equivalent examination along with copy of Transfer Certificate/Migration Certificate in case change of School.
  • Three self attested copies of Mark sheet of 12th Class (10+2) or equivalent Board examination and also copy of Transfer Certificate/Migration Certificate in case change of School/Board.
  • Three self attested copies of Pass Certificate of 12th Class (10+2) or equivalent examination.(showing all the subjects & the name of the school)
  • Three self attested copies of School/College Leaving Certificate for Bihar Board & Tamil Nadu Board Students.
  • Equivalency Certificate from Association of Indian Universities (AIU), New Delhi for the +2 equivalent qualifications, if obtained from abroad along with subject wise equivalency. If marks are given in grades, proof of their equivalent percentage of marks.
  • Three self attested copies of B.Sc. Mark sheet - if the candidate obtained less than 50% marks for General and 40% marks for Reserve Category
  • Three self attested copies of OBC/SC/ST Certificate (mention the Caste Certificate number, date and name and address of the Issuing authority on the back side of copy of the certificate )
  • Three self attested copies of English Translation of OBC/SC/ST Certificate - (if the Certificate is in Regional language) .
  • One additional colour passport size photograph with front view
  • Three attested copies of Admission/Acceptance letter issued by concerned Foreign Medical University
  • Year wise mark sheet of MBBS or equivalent course from 1st year onward, if already taken admission and /or completed the course then copy of degree along with complete mark sheet of MBBS is required.
  • Copy of student ID card issued by the concerned medical university/institute, if admission already taken
  • Additional DD for Verification of 10+2 mark sheet/Certificate, as per list attached with the application form.
  • Original Certificates for Serial No 6 to Sr. No 15 for verification.
  • Affidavit on Rs.10/- stamp paper duly notarised.

STEPS TO STEP PROCESS FOR MBBS :

  • The student must be 17 years plus as on 31st December of the year in which the admission is being taken.
  • Student must have completed class 12th from a regular board/Open Board.
  • Student must have an aggregate of at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry and Biology and must have English as a compulsory subject if the student is from General category.
  • Student must have an aggregate of at least 40% in Physics, Chemistry and Biology and must have English as a compulsory subject if the student is from SC/ST/OBC.
  • Need to clear NEET from 2018 onward for admission in any country in abroad (Cut Off Marks for 2019 to be updated).
  • Apply for the University/Country in which the student wants to go for.
  • Notarise , Apostille Documents.
  • Once the Offer Letter/Confirmation Letter/Student ID is received apply for MCI Certification by filling up the application form and sending required documents to New Delhi.
  • Apply for Visa.
